Races of Cryptillia

Dwarf

Dwarves are less common than Humans, however, they have a much stronger sense of exploration and have therefore spread to the far reaches of the planet. Their technological level is relatively high when compared to many other races, for example, Dwarves have mastered mining, and to some extent, flying. Dwarves value monetary possessions above all else, preferring to protect gold over family in some cases – if they are called upon to protect family riches, then they are a force to be reckoned with! Dwarves prefer to work alone after they have left their families, exploring and getting to know the world under their own steam.


Modifiers

+1 to Stamina
+1 to Skill
-2 to Speed
