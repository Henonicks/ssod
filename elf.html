Races of Cryptillia

Elf

Elves are tall, stately creatures which are able to use a high level of magic. They are protective of land alone, and believe it to belong to the gods – anyone trying to invade elven land is branded a heretic. Elves are quick to obey the orders of a superior. This isn’t only apparent in a military environment, but also in family environments too, where an elven father is the head of the family. Nobody argues and the father must never abuse this power – it is believed he will be judged in the afterlife if he does. Elves are highly spiritual, many knowing the true meaning of magic – and its horrific side effects! Elves can use magic to defend their homes, shape cities in the sky, and even see a short distance into the future..


Modifiers

-1 to Stamina
+2 to Skill
-2 to Luck
+1 to Speed
