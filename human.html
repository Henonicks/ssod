Races of Cryptillia

Human

Humans are the most prolific race on Cryptillia, mostly medieval in technology, passive and protective of material possessions. Humans will defend their homelands from invasion and oppression until they are overcome, and to the most part, have a strong sense of honour and pride. Humans prefer to stay in small social groups such as families, societies and guilds.


Modifiers

+1 to Stamina
+1 to Skill
-1 to Sneak
-1 to Speed
