Races of Cryptillia

Lesser Orc

Lesser Orcs are related genetically to Orcs in some way, however the point at which they diverged is unknown. They share many similar features, such as blue-green blood based on copper instead of iron, three-jointed legs, and extremely thick ribcages, which are able of resisting heavy damage. Lesser Orcs are much smaller than Orcs, and with this decrease in size, appear to inherit a level of cowardice and a passive nature that an Orc would simply be castrated for having! Due to this, they are often used as slaves by Orcs, as they will not resist. Some Lesser Orcs are able to show a base level of courage however, and will rebel and escape, if in captivity. These Lesser Orcs are destined to become soldiers, adventurers, and hired protection of all kinds, if they can hope to live very long at all!


Modifiers

-2 to Stamina
-2 to Skill
+2 to Luck
+2 to Sneak
