The Demon Garneth

The Demon Garneth is by far the most dangerous advisory in the land of Utopia, if not all of Cryptillia at the current time. Garneth was once a powerful lord, ruling a large section of south utopia. Unfortunately, the Lord Garneth became corrupted by the forces of evil. The real reason for his corruption has never been determined, however it is commonly believed to have been his lust for power and a desire to claim more land for himself. Over time, the demon lord has become more powerful, barricading himself in his castle and not permitting anyone to leave alive - Eventually people learned to stay clear of his accursed area. The Lord Garneth has not been seen by anyone alive since the time of his possession, some fifty years ago, as he will never leave the castle. However, his minions are always to be seen terrorising the immediate area, reaching as far as the Larret bridge in the north, and as far as Port Obligan to the south. To this date, there has been no-one courageous enough to confront Garneth and end his reign of terror once and for all...
