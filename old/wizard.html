Professions of Cryptillia

Wizard

Wizards spend most of their lives learning magical spells and how to mix potions. Magic in Cryptillia is all a matter of experience alone – anyone and everyone has magical abilities, however bringing these abilities to bear takes years of practice and meditation, and those who are not aware of the dangers of spell casting can soon be lost to their own powers, or worse. Magic is primarily drawn from latent energy in the surrounding environment, the process of spell casting being to take raw energy, for example heat from fire or kinetic energy from movement, and to mould it into a more useful form using the mind. How this is achieved is not yet known to the people of Cryptillia. However, there are several kinds of creature whose metabolism is completely energy based, such as demons and other apparitions. These are extremely harmful, and capable of possessing a matter-based creature by transferring their energies into its own. If this occurs, then the demon gains complete control – in a wizard this can be very dangerous and usually devastating!
