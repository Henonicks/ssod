Professions of Cryptillia

Woodsman

Woodsmen are trained in the arts of infiltration, exploration and scouting. Not only can they travel expertly through wooded areas, knowing what plants to eat (and what not to eat!) but they are also efficient at travelling in general, knowing how to carefully ration their supplies, and when it is best to travel in certain environments, conserving strength for later. Woodsmen have limited spell casting abilities, preferring to use their own stealthiness and tactics they can understand physically instead of abstract theories needed to effectively use magic.


Modifiers

+2 to Luck
+1 to Speed

Special:


Tracking skill (A woodsman may follow other characters that he has seen in game)
