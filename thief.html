Professions of Cryptillia

Thief

Many of Cryptillia’s occupants are driven to crime to survive. There are many types of criminal in Cryptillia, however petty criminals such as thieves are the most common by far. Thieves are experts at gaining access to locked rooms, and learn enough magic to be able to cast related spells, such as ‘open’. Thieves are also very sneaky, and most have practice at picking pockets as well as locks! Thieves can make good fighters, especially in a battle, as they can scout ahead, sneaking past heavily guarded outposts without being spotted. Thieves also have much more money than is first apparent.


Modifiers

+3 to Sneak

Special:


Pick item (A thief may select the item they wish to steal from other players, instead of getting a random item)
